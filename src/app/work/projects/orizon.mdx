## Overview

Orizon is a pioneering AI-driven decision system designed specifically for the complex risk-management needs of modern enterprises. In today's highly interconnected corporate environments, risk is multifaceted and data is often siloed due to strict privacy regulations and security concerns. Recognizing the limitations of centralized data processing and single-modality AI, I conceptualized and built Orizon around the core principles of Federated Learning and Multi-Modal Artificial Intelligence.

This platform empowers organizations to securely analyze disparate, highly sensitive data sources—ranging from unstructured textual documents and emails to structured time-series metrics and operational logs—without ever compromising data privacy or transferring raw data over external networks. By shifting the computational paradigm from moving data to the models, to moving models to the data, Orizon delivers intelligent, proactive risk mitigation on a global scale.

## The Architecture & Technology Stack

The engineering behind Orizon was driven by the need for absolute security, real-time performance, and scalable machine learning operations.

- **Federated Learning Infrastructure**: Implemented a decentralized training topology using Python and PyTorch. Instead of a central data lake, edge nodes train local models on proprietary data, and only the cryptographic weight updates are aggregated centrally. This ensures zero-trust compliance with stringent data governance laws.
- **Multi-Modal AI Engine**: Engineered a hybrid neural architecture capable of ingesting diverse data types. Natural Language Processing (NLP) pipelines parse risk factors from corporate communications, while deep sequence models evaluate operational metrics, fusing them into a unified risk score.
- **Next.js & React Frontend**: The complex, data-heavy interfaces were built using Next.js and React, prioritizing fluid data visualization and responsive dashboards that can render thousands of data points without UI blocking.
- **Real-Time WebSocket Sync**: Leveraged WebSocket protocols to stream real-time updates regarding federated node connectivity and alert generation directly to the client dashboard.

## Key Features & Business Impact

- **Predictive Risk Scoring**: Translates complex, multi-modal data inferences into an easily digestible 1-100 risk score, allowing executives to identify vulnerable sectors before issues escalate.
- **Federated Status Monitoring**: Provides IT and Security teams with real-time visibility into the health, sync status, and contribution weight of distributed training nodes.
- **Automated Alert Inbox & Document Review**: Uses AI to automatically categorize, prioritize, and flag risk alerts. The system highlights specific paragraphs or metrics in source documents, drastically reducing human review time.
- **Sector & Entity Drilldown**: Offers a granular, hierarchical view of risk. Managers can start at a global sector overview and drill down into specific regional entities, tracing the predictive confidence scores back to historical trends.

## The Vision

Orizon represents the future of secure corporate intelligence. It proves that organizations do not have to choose between robust data privacy and state-of-the-art predictive capabilities. By continuously refining the federated aggregation algorithms and expanding the supported data modalities, Orizon is positioned to become the central nervous system for enterprise risk management.
