## Overview

Orizon is a pioneering AI-driven decision system designed specifically for the complex risk-management needs of modern enterprises. Recognizing the limitations of centralized data processing and single-modality AI, I conceptualized and built Orizon around the principles of Federated Learning and Multi-Modal AI.

This platform allows organizations to securely analyze disparate, sensitive data sources—ranging from textual documents to structural time-series data—without compromising data privacy, ultimately driving intelligent, proactive risk mitigation.

## Key Features

- **Federated Learning Architecture**: Enables secure, decentralized training of AI models across various enterprise silos without ever sharing the raw sensitive data.
- **Multi-Modal AI Engine**: Capable of ingesting and cross-analyzing different data types (text, metrics, operational logs) for a holistic view of potential risks.
- **Dashboard Overview**: A central hub providing real-time risk scoring, predictive trend analysis, and actionable insights at a glance.
- **Alert Inbox & Document Review**: Automated categorization and prioritization of risk alerts, backed by deep document analysis workflows.
- **Sector View & Entity Drilldown**: Deep dive into specific departments, sectors, or external entities to trace the root cause of predicted risks.

## The Vision

Orizon represents the future of secure corporate intelligence. By moving the model to the data rather than the data to the model, Orizon ensures compliance with strict data governance while still benefiting from state-of-the-art predictive capabilities.
